Alternative Options Considered

PayU India Established Player

One of India's oldest payment gateways with wide coverage.

Pros

  • Established brand
  • Good UPI support
  • EMI options
  • Multi-currency

Cons for TipsyDrive

  • Dashboard less intuitive
  • Payout integration separate
  • Documentation not as good
  • Less developer-friendly
Cashfree Growing Alternative

Fast-growing payment gateway with competitive pricing.

Pros

  • Slightly lower pricing (1.9%)
  • Faster settlements (T+1)
  • Good payout system
  • Auto-collect feature

Cons for TipsyDrive

  • Smaller market presence
  • Less brand recognition
  • React Native SDK less mature
  • Split payments less flexible

Future consideration: Cashfree is a solid backup option if Razorpay pricing becomes unfavorable.

Stripe Global Standard

World's most developer-friendly payment platform.

Pros

  • Best developer experience
  • Excellent documentation
  • Great for international
  • Advanced fraud detection

Cons for TipsyDrive

  • UPI support limited in India
  • Local wallets not supported
  • Forex charges for INR
  • Not ideal for India-only MVP
PhonePe Payment Gateway Walmart-Backed

PhonePe's B2B payment gateway solution.

Pros

  • Strong UPI expertise
  • Large user base
  • Competitive pricing
  • Growing platform

Cons for TipsyDrive

  • Newer as gateway
  • Less feature-rich
  • Payout system less mature
  • Smaller developer ecosystem

Bottom Line

For TipsyDrive's payments - requiring UPI, cards, and driver payouts in India - Razorpay provides:

  • All Indian payment methods (UPI, cards, wallets) in one integration
  • RazorpayX for automated driver payouts to bank accounts
  • Route for splitting payments between platform and drivers
  • Excellent React Native SDK for in-app payments
  • Reliable webhooks for payment confirmation
  • Market leader with trusted brand in India
